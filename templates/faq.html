Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common queries about QueMaster.

How does QueMaster generate questions automatically?

We use GPT-based AI to suggest questions from your selected topic and difficulty. You can approve, edit, or regenerate suggestions before finalizing.

Can I choose marks and exam pattern?

Yes. Pick from presets (25/50/100) or define custom rules like “attempt any 4 of 5”, difficulty mix, and section-wise marks.

Is it aligned with NEP and SPPU format?

Yes. We structure papers to match SPPU style and NEP’s competency focus. You can tweak headers, instructions, and sections.

Can I export as PDF?

Absolutely. The final paper is a clean, print‑ready PDF you can download and share.

Will my data be secure?

Passwords are hashed, sessions are protected, and data sits in a secure database. Only you can access your papers.
